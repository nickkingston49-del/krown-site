Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our roofing and construction services in Southeast Texas.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes. Krown Roofing and Construction is fully insured and bonded, so you have peace of mind on every residential and commercial project.

Do you offer free estimates?

We do. Tell us what you need through our contact form or give us a call at (409) 719-2053 and we will get you a free, no-pressure estimate — the same day when we can.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We offer flexible financing through Enhancify so you can start your project sooner and pay over time.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Nederland, Beaumont, Port Neches, Groves, Orange, Lumberton and the surrounding Golden Triangle of Southeast Texas.

Do you handle both residential and commercial work?

Yes. From single-family homes to commercial buildouts like our daycare renovation, we handle projects of all sizes.

What kind of shingles do you use?

We install quality architectural (dimensional) shingles with up to a 30-year warranty, and we will walk you through your options before any work begins.

Can you help with storm or insurance damage?

Yes. We respond quickly after a storm, document the damage, and rebuild it back stronger — roof, drywall and floors.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in one to three days depending on the size of the roof and the weather. We will give you a clear timeline with your estimate.

Do you clean up after the job?

Always. We protect your property during the work and clean up thoroughly when we are done, including a nail sweep of the yard and driveway.

Still have a question?

We are happy to help — reach out and we will get right back to you.
