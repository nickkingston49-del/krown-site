About Krown Roofing and Construction

Owner-operated roofing and remodeling, built on honest work across Southeast Texas.

Krown Roofing and Construction is a locally owned, owner-operated company based in Nederland, Texas, serving Beaumont and the entire Golden Triangle. When you call Krown, you deal directly with the owner — not a call center and not an out-of-town storm chaser. The person who quotes your job is the same person standing behind it when the work is done.

We handle the whole house — roof to floor, inside and out. From roof repairs and full replacements to kitchen and bathroom remodels, painting, decks, fences, and gutters, our crew brings the same standard to every job: quality materials, clean work, and clear communication from the first estimate to the final walk-through.

What we stand for

  • Owner on every job. A direct line to the owner from estimate to completion — no runaround.
  • Insured & bonded. Peace of mind on every project, residential or commercial.
  • Quality materials, real warranties. Architectural shingles with up to 30-year coverage and workmanship we stand behind.
  • Flexible financing. Straightforward estimates with financing through Enhancify so you can start sooner.
  • Clean and on time. We protect your property, clean up after ourselves, and finish when we say we will.

Serving the Golden Triangle

We proudly serve Nederland, Beaumont, Port Neches, Groves, Orange, Lumberton and the surrounding Southeast Texas communities — and we respond fast, especially after a storm. Whether it is a single repair or a full renovation, we treat your home like it is on our own street.
